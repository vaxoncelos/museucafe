about us

Museu Cafe is a fresh place with fresh food. Our menu is seasonal, and we love to use local produce from local farmers, prepared by our amazing Chef Pedro Delgado. Placed in one of the main squares of Funchal, but where you can still relax and not feel overwhelmed by the city traffic! The Sacred Art Museum definitely deserves a visit!
